What You'll Learn

This is the only dental continuing education course that will show you not only how to acquire niche, high-dollar cases but also teach you and your team how to convert them.

Day 1 - Big Case Strategy & Treatment Acceptance

Part 1 - Strategy Development & Business Planning

7:30 AM – 8:00 AM

Registration and Breakfast (Continental Breakfast will be provided).

8:00 AM – 8:20 AM

Welcome and Introductions.

8:20 AM – 10:00 AM

Entreprenuer Goal-Oriented Mindset - Assemble a step-by-step process to alleviate yourself from the limiting mindsets that contribute to failed marketing campaigns and stagnant practices. Learn how and why most marketing campaigns were doomed before they ever began.

10:00 AM – 11:00 AM

Changes in the Dental Marketplace - Recognize trends clinicians encounter within your specific area and construct a strategy to best position your practice to take advantage of the trends in the future. A critical component of practice growth is your ability to anticipate what will happen next and create a strategy. This session will discuss the critical difference between thinking in terms of strategy vs tactics.

11:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Current State Assessment - Unlock the four challenges that practices face in creating a clear and defined strategy. Determine the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats of your practice and how to leverage your practice SWOT for ultimate success. This will help prioritize the timeline in which your strategy will be executed. This is a great team learning exercise.

12:00 PM – 1:00 PM

Lunch (Lunch will be provided).

Part 2 - Case Acceptance for Big Cases

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Introduction: Do we sell in our practices? Do people feel “sold”? We will talk about the key components to an educational and compelling treatment planning process focused on large fee-for-service cases generated direct from the public.

2:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Psychology of Patient Influence - Architect styles to pre-qualify and schedule new patient appointments over the phone. Discern how to pivot the patient’s perception from being focused on price to being focused on the value of your practice. Role-play scenarios of new patients calling in about dental implant cost. This session is extremely interactive for all team members including doctors. You will learn to influence!

3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Personality Profiling and Gaining Trust Instantly - Attain knowledge on a common language that you and your team can use to better understand each other and patients. Elevate sales skills by identifying the patient’s buying motivation and the manner in which they consume information and make decisions.

4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Heuristics - Learn the simple and efficient rules which people often use to form judgements and make decisions. Discover how to utilize mental shortcuts to simplify and streamline the patient education process to increase conversion by focusing on the patient’s “primary desired outcome.” Role-play scenarios for doctors and the clinical team will teach how to find the patient's primary desired outcome and achieve treatment acceptance through heuristics.

Day 2 - Acquisition

Part 1 - Internal Marketing

7:30 AM – 8:00 AM

Registration and Breakfast (Continental Breakfast will be provided).

8:00 AM - 9:30 AM

Make Marketing Simple - Your patients’ experience lays the groundwork for every marketing initiative for your practice. Find ways to effectively showcase your Unique Patient Experience to establish a baseline, ultimately increasing conversions online, and attracting the right type of patients. Most of dental professionals market price-based procedures, specials, or free consultations. None of which are compelling… The most compelling aspect of your practice revolves around the patient's experience, their clinical outcomes, and the increased quality of life that they benefit from. This session will teach you how to capture those experiences within your practice every day and utilize them to generate high-quality cases.

9:30 AM - 10:30 AM

Internal Marketing Execution - Breakout to formulate goals, designate daily trigger points, and organize an execution plan to simplify the creation and execution of your new internal marketing system within the practice.

10:30 AM - 11:30 PM

Marketing Realities - Dissect the top mistakes that are made by marketers today. Break out with your team to create a predictable and realistic marketing strategy, forecast, budget, and method of tracking, based on the current state and goals of your practice.

11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Lunch (Lunch will be provided).

Part 2 - External Marketing

12:30 PM - 1:30 PM

External Digital Media - Compare and analyze a variety of websites to define the 6 most critical factors for an effective landing page that converts new patients for high end, surgical cases. We make this easy and simple!

1:30 PM - 2:30 PM

Directional Media - Appeal to patients that are aware of their dental needs and are searching for experienced clinicians in their area. Discover the top strategies to launch a variety of different digital campaigns for specific cases like implants, laser, and complex restorative procedures.

2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Creative Media - Target the patients that are not aware they have a need for your service. With the right message, a practice can use creative media to increase niche, fee-for-service cases. Explore proven and successful creative media marketing examples such as patient seminars, newspaper, radio, TV, direct mail and more.

3:30 PM - 5:00 PM

Dive into real Progressive Dental case studies of numerous marketing campaigns to identify the factors that determined the campaign's successes and failures. We will use a variety of case studies for you to learn from. Different markets, practice specialties, goals, and budgets will all vary to give you a great perspective and a look behind the curtain!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Catalyst right for me?

Whether you are a general dentist, periodontist, oral surgeon, prosthodontist, orthodontist or any other type of specialist, Catalyst is the dental continuing education course for you. Catalyst is not looking for someone looking to sit through a boring lecture style seminar, this hands-on dental CE will leave you with a plan of action!

Will I get any value out of Catalyst?

Progressive Dental works with some of the highest preforming practices in the country. Even if you have been marketing your dental practice or using dental consulting services for over 20 years, you will benefit from the refinements that we’ve discovered over the years to help accelerate your growth. We will share real case studies of marketing strategies that were successful and other campaigns that failed and why.

Why do I need to attend a live event?

Sure, there are many online dental continuing education courses that you can take to obtain your CE credits for the year. Catalyst is such a unique dental CE course that you cannot replace it with a recording. You and your team members will enter into an environment of success. These two days are full of role plays and execution systems to ensure that you are able to incorporate the systems in your practice the next day! This is your opportunity to be in a room surrounded with the most successful people in the dental industry.

Who should I bring from my team?

Everyone! We always recommend bringing the entire team, as they are all part of the new patient and treatment planning process. We know that each practice has their own circumstances, so if you are unable to bring your entire team, you should bring the key team members, office managers, treatment plan coordinators and lead hygienists that will be able to relay the information to the others.

What if I'm new to this?

Many of our Catalyst attendees are have no prior experience in marketing or creating practice growth strategies. By attending Catalyst, you will learn proven systems to successfully establish goals, develop execution systems, and launch strategies to reach your maximum potential.
